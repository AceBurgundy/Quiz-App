      Game Instructions

    • - Rearrange the random words by clicking on each letter of the randomized characters.
    • - The clicked letters will be placed in an array.
    • - If the letters in the array match the word, you get a point.
    • - You have the option to reshuffle all words by clicking on the reshuffle button.
    • - You can read the description of the game by clicking on the guide button.
    • - You can close the game or skip a word by clicking on the respective buttons.
    • - Have fun playing quizMe!
